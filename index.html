Fun whale facts!
This is a sperm whale. Sperm whales have been recorded descending to depths of over 3,280 feet and can go 90 minutes between breaths.
This is a narwhal. Unlike some whale species that migrate, narwhals spend their lives in the Arctic waters of Canada, Greenland, Norway and Russia.
This is a blue whale. Blue whales are the largest creatures ever to have lived on Earth. Newborn blue whales weigh about the same as an adult hippopotamus.
This is a humpback whale. Humpback whales have some of the longest migrations of any mammal with some populations swimming 5,000 miles between breeding and feeding grounds.
